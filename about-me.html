About Me

Great Design Isn’t Just My Job… It’s Also My Passion!

As a Designer–Technical Designer hybrid, I am thoroughly knowledgeable in both the creative and the technical aspects of Fashion Design and Product Development... blending digital proficiency along with old-fashioned creative thinking and analog skills.

I am fortunate to have worked with some amazing clients, ranging from smaller niche brands to industry icons, including CK Knits, Ralph Lauren, Karl Lagerfeld, and Ann Taylor LOFT. When it comes to understanding your needs, capturing the essence of your brand is at the very heart of what I do.
